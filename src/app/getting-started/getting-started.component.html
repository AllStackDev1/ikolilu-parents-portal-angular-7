Welcome to iKolilu Parents Portal.

1. To add a student, Click the DASHBOARD on the left pane.

2. Then Click on the ADD NEW WARD button.



3. On the dialog, Enter your ward's Student ID number (Can be found on the student's bill), and choose your ward's school from the dropdown.


Then Click ADD STUDENT at the bottom of dialog.


Your ward should now appear at the top of Add My Ward Button. Else please contact your ward's school with your credentials.



To view Bills and Payments for a ward,

1. Click the DASHBOARD on the left.

2. Then Click on the particular ward's ROW .


3. On the next screen, Kindly select Bills / Payments on the left panel.


4. Then select the Class and Term for which you want to see the bills, then click view.


Total Bills, Payments and Balances are highlighted right under the class and term buttons.

5. Under that are two tabs for both Bills and Payments. Click on Payments for Payment records.
For Bills Tab, the Student's bill items are displayed by Description and Amount to be paid.

Under Payments tab, records of all payments made for that class and term are displayed by Receipt number, Date and Amount paid.

To view Notices and Newsletters for a ward,

1. Click the DASHBOARD on the left.

2 .All Notices published by the School for your ALL ward are listed here by date. Please tap on "Details" to view details.
